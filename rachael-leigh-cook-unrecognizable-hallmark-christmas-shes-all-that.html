RACHAEL Leigh Cook has excited fans with a major new movie announcement.

The She's All That star showed off her stunning beauty almost three decades after the '90s hit film.

In a new post on Instagram, Rachael, 43, gave some glimpses of her new Christmas movie on Hallmark, Rescuing Christmas.

The television star looked completely different from her time as nerd-turned-prom queen in She's All That, with long, wavy hair and a full face of flawless makeup.

In one of the pics, Rachael sat at home and made a Christmas tree out of toilet roll, while another showed her next to Santa Claus at a festive market.

One shot showed her looking disgruntled while holding a plastic fox, while the presence of Hallmark hunk Sam Page in the background suggested romance could be in the cards.

Although Rachael is all grown up since her She's All That days, the star was brought back in for a long-awaited reboot.

After the success of She's All That, Netflix aired a new version in 2021.

In the new film, He's All That, a young man undergoes a glow-up makeover.

The movie starred Addison Rae, with its trailer unveiled on TikTok.

Kourtney Kardashian also showed off her acting props, while Rachael herself snagged a starring role.

Rachael played Anna, the mom of Addison Rae's main character, Padgett.

HOT MAMA

Alongside playing a mom on screen, Rachael is a mom in real life, but has split from her children's father.

In 2021, Rachael and her ex-husband Daniel Gillies finalized their divorce two years after they split.

A Los Angeles-based judge signed off on the divorce on March 10, 2021, according to documents obtained by Us Weekly.

The case was resolved in mediation, while the legal document detailed the exes’ plans for their children and pets as they move forward.

Neither party was set to receive spousal support, and the former couple agreed to share joint physical and legal custody of their children: Charlotte, nine; and Theo, seven.
