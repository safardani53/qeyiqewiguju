Eve Engaged: Singer Marrying British Designer Maximillion Cooper

Eve, 35, said yes to her guy’s proposal on Christmas Day, Dec. 25, her rep told E! News.

[hl_youtube src=”https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UqUmDVjB4n0″ link=”https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UqUmDVjB4n0″ text=”Eve — Eve ft. Miss Kitty”]

The pair splits their time together between the United States and Britain, and they have been together for over 2 years, according to E! It is the first time Eve will be getting married, but Maximillion has four children from his previous marriage.

Maximillion is the entrepreneur behind edgy lifestyle brand Gumball 3000, and Eve has had her fair share of successful clothing collaborations and designs, so they are a real match!

Eve Shows Off Giant Engagement Ring

Eve showed off the first glimpse of her huge diamond ring in a family photo she posted to Instagram on Dec. 30. She updated her fans that she was spending New Year’s Eve in England with her family, writing: “#familytime!!!! Me and my mom and my lil brother Farrod just got to the English country side. Feel so#blessed to be with my family and sharing my life here.”

Judging by the huge smile on Eve’s face, we’d say she’s over the moon about her engagement news! She passed on the exciting energy to her fans as well, writing “I pray that you bring 2014 in with people you #love. And nothing but #good #energy ❤️❤️❤️❤️.”
