Love &amp; Hip Hop Atlanta Reunion Brawl: Was Joseline on Crack?!

Publish date: 2024-06-18

Was the Love & Hip Hop Atlanta reunion fight that broke out earlier this summer – and we saw our first VH1 glimpses of Monday – triggered by crack cocaine use?

A massive lawsuit alleges that one of the stars was on crack at the reunion, and if you watch Love & Hip Hop Atlanta online, this is not an outrageous claim.

Althea Eaton has filed suit against her co-star Joseline Hernandez, who is seen on tape punching Althea in the face and dragging her to the ground.

Benzino’s fiancee, Althea says she was hit, scratched and pummeled by Joseline, Stevie J’s non-wife and a frequent stirrer of the L&HH:A pot.

According to Althea’s lawsuit, witnesses allegedly observed Joseline smoking crack out of a pipe backstage during the show, which was taped in New York.

Althea believes Joseline was high as all getout when the Love & Hip Hop Atlanta reunion fight broke out, and should be held accountable for her actions.

 

For what it’s worth, Eaton suing the reality show or the network, just Hernandez.

The tension was palpable on its own. You could cut it with a knife in there … and soon enough, we’ll see the cast members possibly try to literally cut each other.
