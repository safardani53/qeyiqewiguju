How many children did Tammy Wynette have?

Publish date: 2024-07-31

ALTHOUGH Tammy Wynette had several stepchildren brought in from her various husbands, she only had four daughters.

Here we take a look at all her children and see if any of them followed in their famous mother's footsteps.

Who are Tammy Wynette's children?

Gwendolyn Lee

Gwendolyn Lee Byrd is the eldest daughter of Tammy Wynette and her first husband Euple Byrd.

She was born on April 14, 1961.

Despite the limelight enjoyed by her mother, Gwendolyn has stayed away from the cameras for most of her life.

Not much is known about the life of Gwendolyn, apart from the fact that she is Tammy Wynette’s oldest daughter.

Jackie Daly

Jackie Daly is the second daughter of Tammy and Euple Byrd.

Jackie Daly was born on August 21, 1962.

Unlike her elder sister, Jackie is active on social media.

Every now and then, she shares pictures of herself with her starlet mom Tammy Wynette and sisters on the social media platform Facebook.

Tina Denise Byrd

Tina Denise Byrd is the youngest daughter of Tammy and Euple Byrd.

She was born on March 27, 1965.

Tina briefly ventured into the limelight at just eight years old when she worked on the album titled, George and Tammy and Tina with her parents.

Many songs from the album landed on the Billboard charts.

However, as she grew up, Tina didn’t stay in touch with the limelight.

Georgette Jones

Georgette Jones is the only child of Tammy and George Jones.

The pair met each other on tour and exchanged their vows in the year 1969.

They welcomed their first and only child daughter Georgette Jones in October 1970.

Georgette followed in the footsteps of her parents and decided to pursue a career in music.

She was just 10 years old when she recorded her first song titled Daddy Come Home with her father George.

Over the years, she has released five albums and toured internationally as a country artist.

How many times was Tammy Wynette married?

Tammy was married five times throughout her life.

Tammy Wynette was married to Euple Byrd for five years, Don Chapel for one year, George Jones for six years, Michael Tomlin for 2 months, and George Richey for 20 years.

The singer was married to Richey up until her death on April 6, 1998.
