Brief Glance on Vasyl Lomachenko

Vasyl Lomachenko (born on 17 February 1988) was previously an ice hockey player. Likewise, he initially played as an ice hockey player during his early years and even thought of advancing it professionally. 

However, since his father used to be a professional boxing coach, he caught that line and went for it.

Also, he only entered the Ring after learning traditional Ukrainian dance and gymnastics.
