    When is Black Friday 2021?

    This year, Black Friday is happening on November 26.

    That's the day immediately after U.S. Thanksgiving, just like every year - Black Friday being an American export.

    While it's been going strong in the States since the Sixties, it's only grown in popularity in the last decade or so in the UK.

    Now, though, it's a permanent fixture in the calendar, and it's when you'll find the very best discounts - so best get the 26th in your diary!

    When is Cyber Monday 2021?

    Cyber Monday always happens after the weekend that immediately follows Black Friday.

    So this year, it's happening on Monday November 29.

    It's got the (slightly old-fashioned-sounding) name as historically, Cyber Monday was a day of online-only sales - most shoppers having exhausted themselves dashing around the stores on Black Friday itself.

    These days, however, there's very little difference between the two sales days, as you'll rarely find any deals that are only available in-store.

    Cyber Monday is generally when retailers send out their final wave of special offers, and reduce prices even further on their remaining stock.

    So if you see a fantastic tablet deal on Black Friday, there's always a chance it could be further reduced when Cyber Monday comes around - but there's an equal chance it sells out before then.

    Our advice? Don't wait.
