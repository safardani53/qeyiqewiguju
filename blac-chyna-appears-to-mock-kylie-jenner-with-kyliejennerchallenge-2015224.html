Blac Chyna Appears to Mock Kylie Jenner With #KylieJennerChallenge

Publish date: 2024-04-27

Did she just? Wait, maybe she did. Tyga’s ex-girlfriend Blac Chyna caused a stir on Tuesday, April 21, when she appeared to take the viral #KylieJennerChallenge via Twitter.

Blac Chyna, 26, posed for a selfie as she sprawled out on the floor with huge Wack-O-Wax red lips on her face. In a second snapshot, she posted a close-up of the plastic piece. “Will be available on @88_fin,” she wrote.

The model didn’t follow the social media trend exactly, but she did grab attention from followers. Those who tried to mimic Jenner’s lips stuck their mouths in a small cup or shot glass — at their peril! — until their skin would swell.
