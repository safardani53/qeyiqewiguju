Hunter Renfrow Age, Biography

Hunter Renfrow was born on December 21, 1995, in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, USA. James Hunter Renfrow is his given name. He is of American nationality and of American-White ethnicity. He is the youngest of six children born to Suzanne and Tim Renfrow. Tim, his father, is one of eleven children. He graduated from Clemson with his brother, Jordan. He is a devout Christian. As of 2023, he is 27 years old.

His father was the football coach at Socastee High School in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. He was a two-star recruit according to Rivals.com and played quarterback for the team. He later enrolled at Clemson University and became a member of the Clemson Tigers football team.
