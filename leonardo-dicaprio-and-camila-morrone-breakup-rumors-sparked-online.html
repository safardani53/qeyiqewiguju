Leonardo DiCaprio And Camila Morrone Breakup Rumors Sparked Online

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Leonardo DiCaprio and Camila Morrone recently made headlines when insiders close to the couple informed People magazine that they had chosen to end their four-year relationship. The revelation comes after the now-ex-couple was seen vacationing together in Malibu during the Fourth of July holiday, soon after Morrone turned 25. Another insider told E! News that DiCaprio and Morrone recently “traveled a lot” and decided to “re-evaluate their relationship.” The source also said that “distance played a role” in the breakup and that the “relationship ran its run.”

As word of Leonardo DiCaprio and Camila Morrone’s supposed divorce spread, netizens reacted with a flood of humorous memes showcasing DiCaprio’s alleged refusal to date any woman older than 25, as per his relationship history. DiCaprio and Morrone began dating over six months after meeting at the 2017 Cannes Film Festival, despite their 22-year age difference. An insider told People magazine in July 2019 that the pair was “very serious” about each other:

“This is not a casual connection.” Camila spends a significant amount of time in his home. Camila has long been rumored to be Leo’s girlfriend. And Leo long ago introduced her to both of his parents.”

Another source said in June 2020 that the pair was “extremely close to one other” and spent a lot of time together throughout the COVID-19 epidemic. Unfortunately, their relationship is said to have ended early this month.

Netizens react to the rumored split of Leonardo DiCaprio and Camila Morrone

Leonardo DiCaprio has a lengthy history of dating younger women and supposedly breaking up with them before they reach the age of 25. The notion emerged when the actor from Don’t Look Up began dating Brazilian model Gisele Bundchen. Bundchen was 20 years old at the time, while DiCaprio was almost 25. The couple dated for five years before splitting up in 2005, the year Bundchen turned 25. Soon after, the former started dating Bar Refaeli, a 20-year-old model.

The pair allegedly split up in 2011, just after Refaeli turned 26. DiCaprio subsequently began dating 23-year-old Blake Lively, but the couple split before the actress turned 25. He also had a romance with German model Toni Garrn between 2013 and 2014, however they split up before she turned 26. DiCaprio’s second long-term romance started in 2017 with 19-year-old Camila Morrone. Despite their large age difference, the couple remained together for four years.
