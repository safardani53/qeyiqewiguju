Bryce Mitchell is an American mixed martial artist who currently competes in the Featherweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC).

Hailing from Texarkana, Arkansas, he was born on October 4, 1994. He spent his entire childhood with his parents, playing multiple sports like basketball for the varsity team and wrestling.

Bryce attended Cabot High School and came second in the 7A state championships as a junior. On the other hand, he came fourth at the 6A state championships in his senior year.

The 29 years old later moved to Searcy, enrolled at Harding University, and graduated earning a degree in economics. 

Mitchell, with his undefeated streak in his MMA career, has always had his skills and potential in the sport. 

Mitchell’s UFC debut marked a career milestone, entering one of MMA’s most prestigious promotions.

His participation in The Ultimate Fighter: Undefeated Featherweight tournament has helped him gain immense recognition. 

So far, he boasts an impressive professional record of 15 wins and 1 loss, with 9 wins by submission and 6 by decision.
