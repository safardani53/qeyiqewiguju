Publish date: 2024-08-24

John Arthur Johnson was the full name of one of the American boxer. He is the first African American to become world heavyweight boxing champion. It is the fact that he was the most famous and the most notorious African-American on Earth.

Besides his boxing career, he was involved in the business too. He had opened a luxurious black and tan restaurant and nightclub, which was supported by his wife. He fought for many years and operated numerous other businesses that also include endorsement deals. He is not with us anymore as he died in a car crash when he was only 68.
