'The Goldbergs' Latest News and Updates

Publish date: 2024-02-03

The sitcom The Goldbergs was created by Adam F. Goldberg and based on his childhood and family in Pennsylvania in the '80s. Adam regularly videotaped events during his childhood, and the original tapes are shown in each episode before the end credits. Adult Adam (Patton Oswalt) narrates the show. 

In March 2021, George Segal, who portrayed Adam's grandfather in the series, passed away. His final episode aired on April 7.
