Amanda Wyss is an American film and television actress. She began her career in the early 1980s and first gained notice for her role as Lisa in Fast Times at Ridgemont High (1982). She subsequently starred in Silverado (1985), Better Off Dead (1985), Powwow Highway (1989), and The Id (2016). Wyss is also known for her role as Randi McFarland in the television series Highlander: The Series.
