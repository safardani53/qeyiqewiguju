Today's Savannah Guthrie reunites with co-stars for football game on Sunday- but 3 key hosts are mis

Publish date: 2024-08-08

SAVANNAH Guthrie reunited with three of her Today co-stars at the New York Giants' MetLife Stadium on Sunday.

However, three key hosts were missing from the gathering.

Football season kicked off on Sunday, and the Today hosts got together to celebrate it.

"Great day tailgating for a good cause with Eli Manning + look who we ran into!! @cocogauff joins us Monday morning live on TODAY!" Savannah captioned her post.

The Today host posted a carousel of photos to her Instagram.

Savannah took photos with her co-hosts Hoda Kotb, Craig Melvin, and Al Roker on the football field.

They all wore orange jerseys with the word "Today" and a number on the front.

The hosts attended Eli Manning's charity event, Tackle Kids Cancer.

September is Childhood Cancer Awareness Month.

Savannah also posted photos of Eli posing with children and then more of her hugging tennis player, Coco Gauff.

WHERE ARE THEY?

Despite Al, Savannah, Craig, and Hoda attending the event, some of their co-hosts were left out.

Today third-hour hosts, Dylan Dreyer and Sheinelle Jones, were not in attendance.

Also, Carson Daly, who has been missing from the morning show for a while, was not at the stadium.

Sheinelle was on the morning show on Monday, but Carson was not.

Dylan was also not on Today that morning.

It is unclear why the other hosts were not present at the charity event.

LEFT OUT AGAIN

Last week, Savannah posted photos of the hosts having fun on set.

However, key hosts were left out again from the group photos.

"Let's go!" she captioned the video.

At the beginning of the video, Hoda and Savannah walked into the set with orange and yellow backpacks with their names on them, on their backs, to celebrate the first day of school.

They headed out onto the Today set and interacted with the other hosts.

Everyone from Craig to Al was seen goofing off and having fun together.

The video ended with the message: "Happy Back to Everything season."

Fans quickly noticed that images of both Sheinelle and Dylan were once again missing.
