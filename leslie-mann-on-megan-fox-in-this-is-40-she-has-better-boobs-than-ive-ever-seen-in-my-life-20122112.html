Leslie Mann on Megan Fox in This is 40: She Has 'Better Boobs Than I've Ever Seen In My Life'

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Leslie Mann was recently granted the privilege of copping a feel of Megan Fox's breasts in her husband Judd Apatow's upcoming comedy This is 40. The verdict?

"Megan just has great boobs," Mann told Yahoo! Movies in a recent interview. "Better boobs than I've ever seen in my life."

In the film, Mann, 40, reprises her role from Knocked Up, playing boutique shop owner boss Debbie to Fox's saleswoman Desi.

During the aforementioned scene, Mann's character stumbles upon Desi trying on a hot pink push-up bra and can't resist admiring her employee's much-perkier breasts.

"Are those real?" she asks, eyes widening. 

"Yeah, I'm just young!" Desi responds.

Debbie then proceeds to grab at Desi in a hilarious scene that Mann later admitted she found a bit awkward.

"I feel very protective of younger actresses because it was so hard for me in the business," she said of Fox, 26. "I wanted to get it over [with] as quickly as possible, because I knew it was uncomfortable for her, probably – or maybe not."

"But just in that situation I knew there were a lot of dirty boys around having weird, gross fantasties. And so I wanted to get her shirt back on as quickly as possible."

One of those "dirty boys," however, was not Mann's husband of 15 years, Apatow, who admitted that in spite of all his films' potty humor, he still felt "uncomfortable" about Fox's shirtlessness.

"I get so uncomfortable with even partial nudity in movies. I'll do one or two takes, and feel weird," he told Yahoo! Movies. "Megan is a beautiful woman, but she literally changes the temperature of a room when she walks in."
