Kanye West Donda Homecoming Chicago: How can I buy tickets?

Publish date: 2024-08-09

KANYE West is back at it again with another Donda event but this time, it's a basketball match between the Donda Doves and Chicago’s Prep Academy.

The game is scheduled for February 5, 2022, and fans want to know where they can get tickets.

How can I buy tickets for Donda Homecoming?

The Donda Homecoming game is scheduled to take place at the Credit Union 1 Arena in Chicago, Illinois.

Ticket prices range from $20 to $500 and are available for purchase on the Ticketmaster website.

Due to the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic, the Event Organizer is requiring all attendees of the event to be fully vaccinated.

Fans will also be required to show proof of vaccination and for those who are 5 years of age or younger, they may be required to show proof of a negative Covid test upon entering.

Who are the Donda Doves?

The Donda Doves are known as the inaugural class of Donda Academy's basketball team. Donda Academy is known as a California-based prep school that is backed by West, 44, himself.

The team debuted in 2022 and graced the cover of SLAM Magazine.

The team members include the elite prospects:

  • Robert Dillingham of Hickory, North Carolina
  • Zion Cruz of Jersey City, New Jersey
  • JJ Taylor of Chicago, Illinois
  • Brandon White of Spencer, North Carolina
  • Omarion Bodrick of Charlotte, North Carolina
  • Seven Bahati of Hacienda Heights, California
  • Bryce Baker or Mooresville, North Carolina
  • Jalen Hooks of Indianapolis, Indiana
  • Chuck Bailey of Detroit, Michigan
  • Braeden Moore of Nashville, Tennessee
  • Jahki Howard of Norcross, Georgia

“Most of us knew each other from Instagram and playing against each other,” Hooks told the media outlet.

Despite being associated with West, many players faced trouble when leaving their respective high school programs as many thought it was for the wrong reasons.

“A lot of my teammates, when they left [their hometowns], they had some bad reactions,” Moore explained.

“People called them clout chasers and said the [school] offered them money.” 

While there are some who may have doubts about West's K12 curriculum, Shayla Scott, the school’s Vice President and Director of Athletics, said that he was “passionate and loyal” when expressing his vision.

“When I have opportunities to talk with [Ye] about his vision for the school, it’s by no stretch of the imagination something that’s out of reach. He wants to do all the things, and big things,” Scott said.

“I get really excited whenever we have a chance to just sit down and talk about what we’re doing and how it’s so minor in some respects to what he wants to do.” 

How much is tuition at Donda Academy?

Donda academy will reportedly have just 60 students for its inaugural year and the tuition is free, according to MTV News.

"Donda Academy prepares students to become the next generation of leaders, thinkers and innovators by providing them with a world-class education," Beulah McLoyd, the school's education consultant, told the media outlet.

In September 2021, the school released its mission statement which reads: "Finding the intersection between faith and the innovation of the future. Donda Academy is focused on equipping students with an education that will last in the ever-changing world."
