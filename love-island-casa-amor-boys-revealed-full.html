Love Islands Casa Amor boys revealed in full - with hot footballer, PT and gorgeous model

Publish date: 2024-02-24

The Casa Amor boys have finally been revealed - and among them are a hot footballer, a hunky trainer and fit model.

Tonight, the girls made their way over to the Casa Amor villa, which has had its sexiest ever makeover.

And once there, they met the six new lads who are hoping to steal their hearts away from the boys back in the main villa.

Here's a look at who's who...

Kodie Murphy

20 year old Kodie is a Social Media Marketer from Birmingham.

Kodie claims he wants to go into Casa Amot as "there isn't as much pressure" as when you are an OG, and describes himself as "cheeky, charismatic, enthusiastic and a bit of a charmer".

As for who he has his eye on, Sammy better watch out.

"Jess is my weak point as I do love a blonde," Kodie admits. "Leah is a very pretty girl but Jess’ personality is stand out for me. She can give good banter!"

His plan to win her over?

"I’m going to go in there and be my big, bubbly, cheeky self," he smiles. " I work well under pressure, so no worries there.

Ouzy See

28 year-old Ouzy is from Edinburgh and has three jobs: footballer, tradie and model.

He's confident too, claiming he will be the one to nab a lady in Casa.

Ouzy says: "I’m excited to see which girls are open to having their heads turned, if anyone could make that happen, it would be me."

He has a link to Ella, having worked with her as a model.

Admitting she would have been the one he would have gone for had he been in the villa from the beginning, Ouzy explains: "I’ve met Ella before on a photoshoot we were both working on and also on nights out in Edinburgh, although nothing has ever happened between us.

He isn't totally laser-focussed though. "Kady interests me too, I watched her on Series 2 and she was a bit of a firecracker," he adds.

Zachary Ashford

Aged 27 from Manchester, Zach works in sales and promises he is in Casa Amor to "stir the pot" and "walk out with a girl I like".

Claiming he has "Northern charm" on his side, Zachary is looking for "loyalty", "humour" and a girl who is "family orientated".

He adds: "That brings a lot of great qualities to a person and something that I am passionate about because I come from a big family."

His main focus in Casa is Jess because he "gets a lot of good vibes from her", but he also calls Leah "beautiful looking" and claims Catherine has "something about her".

As for Kady, he says she is his "natural type" and "someone I would normally go for."

Elom Ahlijah-Wilson

22 year-old Londoner Elom is a masseuse and personal trainer.

"I want to ruffle a few feathers and to see if I can find a strong connection," he teases.

"I don’t beat around the bush, I don’t have a filter. If I don’t like you, I’m going to say it straight up. I’m confident. Looks wise, 10/10. Once I set myself a goal I always achieve it, always. There hasn’t been a time where I haven’t achieved it."

Elom also doesn't believe any of the existing couples will weather the storm.

"I don’t feel like any of them are strong," he insists. "I’ll be real with you. All their heads can be turned at the same time."

Lochan Nowacki

25 year-old Lochan is an Account Manager from Windsor.

"I feel like I’m excited because a lot of the people in relationships aren’t fully secure," he says of heading into Casa Amor. "I know the type of person I am as well, so I feel like I can turn heads."

"I can speak to anyone and hold a conversation. I’m quite a fun person, I like to let loose. I don’t really care so much what other people think about me as long as I’m enjoying myself, being respectful."

His top pick?

"My top is definitely Kady. She seems like a very grounded person, and has that mature side to her but you know she can have a lot of fun. I like her maturity compared to some of the other girls. She’s very much taking her time to build on relationships and speak to people. And she’s obviously gorgeous!"

Benjamin Noel

Aged 26, the fitness business owner from London describes himself as "funny, warm and I pride myself on respect."

He claims he will "thrive naturally" in the pressure cooker that is Casa Amor, and has his sights set on Leah.

"I don’t think she’s met her match yet, the traits she has are what I look for in a girl," he explains.

"I also like Kady, she’s really attractive so I want to figure out if we match in terms of our personalities. Whitney is another girl I’m drawn to, she’s a really cool girl."
