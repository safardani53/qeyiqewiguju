The 11 Best Denim Joggers for Women of 2024

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Dressy and comfortable pants can be hard to come by, but denim joggers offer the perfect solution. They're like a combination of jeans and sweatpants, allowing you to get the best of both worlds, says celebrity stylist Stacee Michelle. “Denim joggers offer the relaxed, easy-wearing comfort of traditional joggers while incorporating the timeless and versatile look of denim,” she tells PEOPLE. “This combination makes them an attractive choice for fashion-forward individuals seeking the best of both worlds in terms of comfort and style.” 

Similar to class denim, denim joggers come in different styles and materials, which can make it hard to find the perfect pair. But don't worry — we've done the research and found the best pairs based on comfort, size, fit, and style, so you can find the option you need without any hassle. We've also considered materials, price, and user reviews in our selection, giving you a better feel for how these joggers perform in real life.

Keep reading to discover the best denim joggers, recommended by PEOPLE.

How To Pick the Right Denim Joggers

Fit 

Whether dress pants, chinos, or jeans, the fit of pants is always an important consideration. And the same goes for denim joggers too, because, as Appelt tells PEOPLE, you'll want to opt for styles that offer a comfortable yet complementary fit. “Look for options with a loose or slim fit, depending on your body type,” she says. “If you're curvier, you may want a slimmer fitting pair that will show off your figure; if you're more of a rectangular or inverted triangle shape, a loose-fitting pair of sweatpants will give you some shape and movement.” 

Appelt adds that the length of your denim joggers is just as important as the style: too long, and they won't flatter your body type. For those who are shorter, stick with ankle-length joggers to ensure the silhouette is not overpowering and to allow for your shoes to make a statement as well.

Fabric 

When it comes to denim joggers, the fabric is like the personality of the joggers — it can greatly affect their performance. So, if you want to be comfortable and move with ease, Appelt recommends choosing a high-quality denim with a blend of stretch material. 

Waistband Width

Jean sweatpants often feature elastic or drawstring waistbands to give them a sporty look, but don't forget to pay attention to the width of the waistband, as it can affect the fit of the sweatpants. Appelt suggests a thicker waistband for full figures to help smooth out the lower torso.

Styling

Appelt also recommends thinking about how you can mix and match denim joggers with other items in your wardrobe. “I always tell my clients to think about three ways they can wear something when making a purchase,” she says. This way, if you only foresee the joggers being feasible in one outfit or look, then you probably won't get a lot of wear out of them and they won't be as worthy of an investment.

Wash and Color 

If you're wondering how to style denim joggers, Appelt recommends paying attention to the wash and color which can really impact the overall vibe of your outfit. “Dark washes are versatile and can be dressed up or down, while lighter washes are more casual,” she explains. Appelt also suggests that distressed or faded options can give a more fashionable look, so it's important to consider your style and how you want to wear them.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are denim joggers called?

    While different fashion brands often create their own names for them, "denim joggers," "denim sweatpants," "denim athletic pants," "denim sweatpants" and "denim jogger jeans" are all names for the same thing, according to fashion stylist Brooke Rounick. She adds that they can also be called "denim sports pants."

  • Are denim joggers worth it?

    To invest or not to invest in denim joggers? That is the question. But if you ask Rounick, the answer is a resounding yes. “Sometimes more expensive options offer benefits such as better fabric quality, attention to detail, and craftsmanship that can contribute to their longevity,” she tells PEOPLE. However, it's not always necessary to spend a lot of money to find a good quality jogger, and there are many options with different prices." (Eh hem —which you can find in this very roundup).

  • What are denim joggers made of?

    Different brands use different materials for their denim joggers, and as such, the price can vary. However, according to Rounick, most joggers are made from denim fabric (no surprise there), which is primarily cotton. However, you'll also find fabric blends including spandex for extra stretch, polyester, rayon, nylon, and lyocell.
