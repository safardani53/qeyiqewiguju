Who Is Amanda Stanton From The Bachelor Alum?

Publish date: 2024-02-17

Her thoughts on the February 21 episode of the dating competition program. Clayton Echard, a 28-year-old former football player, sent one of his dates, Genevieve Parisi, back home on the ABC program because she didn’t appreciate talking about her emotions or getting upset in front of everyone. Amanda Stanton expressed her worries on Instagram on February 22, 2022. She said, ”

“We’ve been watching The Bachelor this season, and I have to admit, something about this season’s several therapy dates is rubbing me the wrong way.” I see where they were trying to go with that… Forcing girls to ‘open up’ on national television and then physically evaluating their sessions or sending them home and making them feel awful if they don’t open up enough is just not the way to go.”

She continued,

“There’s a reason counseling is private, and I don’t believe this is a healthy portrayal of treatment or helping to reduce the stigma around mental health.” “I just felt compelled to express it.”

All About Amanda Stanton

Amanda Stanton shot to fame in 2016 after appearing on the 20th season of The Bachelor. The 31-year-old single mother became an immediate fan favorite. She was, however, eliminated in the eighth week of the program. In 2016, she was featured on season 3 of Bachelor in Paradise and was proposed to by her ex-fiancé Josh Murray. However, the pair called it quits on their engagement and split up later that year. In the fourth season of Bachelor in Paradise, Stanton did not give up hope and continued to look for love. She returned to the program and resumed her relationship with her ex-boyfriend Robby Hayes. But things did not work out with them, and she was upset again.

Stanton, the Lani, the Label creator, was previously involved with pro beach volleyball player Bobby Jacobs and later with Oren Agman, a New York City-based attorney. However, both relationships ended in divorce. Stanton introduced her real estate beau Michael Fogel on Valentine’s Day, 2021; they got engaged on December 2, 2021. Stanton and her children Charlie and Kinsley from ex-husband Nick Buonfiglio exchange Instagram images with Fogel.

Buonfiglio and Stanton divorced in 2015 after three years of marriage. The fashion blogger and Now Accepting Roses author also appeared in the Disney Channel original Cheetah Girls 2 in 2006, the TV movie Merry Christmas, Drake & Josh in 2008, and Kissing Strangers in 2010.
