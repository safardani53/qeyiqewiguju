Could there be a season 2 reunion?

After Netflix provided THTH viewers with a virtual reunion of the season one cast, fans are eager for a series two catch up!

As of yet, it is unclear as to whether or not there will be a Too Hot To Handle season two reunion…

Currently, the COVID-19 pandemic makes things much more complicated but you can count on us to provide you with the latest news if a THTH reunion does appear!

The first series of Too Hot To Handle premiered on Netflix on 17th April 2020, and the reunion was aired on 8th May 2020.

If Netflix choose to follow a similar time scale, this could result in a possible reunion being aired in the next month or so…
