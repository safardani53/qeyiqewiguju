Meet the cast of Farmer Wants a Wife Australia 2023

Publish date: 2024-08-18

FARMER Wants a Wife Australia is back with a fresh batch of hunky men.

Five new farmers are preparing to follow their hearts to find that special someone.

Who stars in Farmer Wants a Wife Australia?

Farmer Brad

Brad is a crop and cattle farmer from Cootamundra, NSW.

He likes to think that he doesn’t take life too seriously but knows how to be serious when needed.

He also coaches a Rugby Union team and is a charity auctioneer.

Brad would like his partner to be his best friend as well as his teammate.

He's a good communicator and has no trouble talking about his feelings.

Farmer David

David is a fourth-generation farmer from Pozieres, QLD.

He is outgoing and enjoys life while building a future on the farm.

When it comes to love, he wants to find the ultimate teammate to do life with.

He is looking to connect with someone on a deeper level.

David believes he would bring a sense of adventure to a relationship.

Farmer Brenton

Brenton is a sixth-generation cattle farmer from Darriman, VIC.

While he can be shy when he meets new people, he is always up for a good time.

He loves country music but has a soft spot for Taylor Swift as well.

Brenton is looking for someone who’s kind and takes pride in herself.

Farmer Matt

Matt is a fifth-generation sheep and cattle farmer from Bookham, NSW.

He's well-travelled with lots of stories to tell and is a hopeless romantic.

When he's not on the farm he volunteers as a firefighter.

Matt believes that the most exciting thing about love is that it can you hit you in the face when you least expect it.

Farmer Andrew

Andrew is a crop and sheep farmer from Narromine, NSW.

He's not your typical farmer as his interest stretch beyond the farm.

In his free time, he goes to Latin and ballroom dancing lessons once a week and plays tennis and golf.

He’s easy-going, fun, loyal, hardworking, engaging and honest.

Andrew believes that love comes when you least expect it.

He’s looking for someone who knows what she wants and is open to self-growth.

How can I watch Farmer Wants a Wife?

Farmer Wants a Wife is available to watch on All4.

New episodes arrive every Friday.

What is Farmer Wants a Wife about?

Farmer Wants a Wife connects farmers with women who aren't having any luck finding a partner in the big city.
