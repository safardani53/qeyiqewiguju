Drew Barrymore and Will Kopelman: Engaged!

Publish date: 2024-05-01

Celebrity Engagement Week continues!

First, Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel. Then, Mario Lopez and Courtney Mazza.

Now, Us Weekly confirms that Drew Barrymore and Will Kopelman have agreed to marry, as the latter popped the most romantic question of all around the holidays during a trip to Idaho.

This will mark the third marriage for the actress, who rose to fame as a child in ET and wed Jeremy Thomas in 1994. They got divorced a month later. She then married Tom Green in July of 2001 and that union ended in December 2001.

We wish this couple at least one full year of happiness together!
