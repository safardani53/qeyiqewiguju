I built a budget friendly pool in my tiny garden that overlooks my street &amp; everyones saying the

Publish date: 2024-02-25

A WOMAN has revealed how she’s transformed her tiny garden by building a budget-friendly swimming pool for her to relax in.

The new attraction - which overlooks her entire street - has already received lots of attention as everyone has been asking the same question. 

TikTok user Marita B. Myklebust, who can be found at @smallhouseliving_no, lives in what she describes as a “tiny house” in Norway. 

She regularly shares videos of her home transformation, including her stylish interiors. 

But now Marita has turned her attention to the outside of her home after she decided to make it summer-friendly.

The social media user recently uploaded a video, which showed how she had upgraded her back garden by installing a pool in the previously rundown space.

Now, she is the proud owner of an impressive decking area with a small pool, summer seating, and chill-out area. 

Alongside a video of the amazing transformation, Marita wrote: “Better? #diypool #backyardvibes”. 

She later posted another clip of how it was made, using wooden beams and a plastic pool, which she described as her “budget friendly pool.” 

Marita’s followers immediately responded as they heaped praise on the DIY lover for the idea.
