Northumberland Plate Day: tips, racecards and previews for the big meeting at Newcastle live on ITV

Publish date: 2024-01-29

IT'S Plate day at Newcastle and this year's renewal looks an absolute belter.

A maximum field of 20 will head to post for the £150,000 handicap over two miles on the all-weather, while there are some high quality supporting races too.

Race 1 - 1.50 Betfair Exchange Handicap

A fiercely competitive sprint to kick things off, but NICK VEDDER looks to have a big chance.

Michael Wigham's five-year-old has a formidable record at this track (yet to finish outside the first three in ten starts here, including three wins), he arrives fresh from a break and remains on a fair mark.

The hat-trick seeking Air Raid is another who brings rock-solid claims to the table, while Brian The Snail and Cosmic Law have the ability to play a part.

At a big price, the well-handicapped Von Blucher shouldn't be overlooked in first-time blinkers.

VERDICT: Win - Nick Vedder Each-way - Von Blucher

Race 2 - 2.25 Chipchase Stakes (Group 3)

Things didn't go to plan for INVINCIBLE ARMY in the Diamond Jubilee Stakes last weekend.

But he sets a good standard under his penalty, is 1-1 on the all-weather and will be tough to beat if anywhere near his best.

Mr Lupton is the obvious threat following his impressive win at the Curragh, but he will need to improve again to win this.

Four of Encrypted's five career wins have come on the all-weather, so it would be no surprise to see him figure in the finish.

VERDICT: Win - Invincible Army Each-way - Encrypted

Race 3 - 3.00 Betfair Exchange Northumberland Vase Handicap

CARWENNAN has progressed well this term for up-and-coming trainer Charlie Fellowes.

The four-year-old won tidily at York before following up at Chelmsford, and their is a strong suspicion he is better than his current mark.

Coeur De Lion ran an excellent race at Royal Ascot last week and he ought to play a part, provided that tough contest hasn't left a mark.

Canford Heights will have come on plenty for his comeback run at Kempton, while bottom-weight Always Resolute stays all day and has a squeak.

VERDICT: Win - Carwennan Each-way - Canford Heights

Race 4 - 3.35 Betfair Exchange Northumberland Plate Handicap

Plenty in with chances. Withhold was gifted an easy lead when landing this 12 month ago, but he is 8lb higher this time around and he won't be left alone up top this time.

The well-backed jolly Gibbs Hill commands respect, but he has been off for 724 days and is 11lb higher than when winning at Kempton in July 2017. He is also unproven at the trip.

Winning machine King's Advice should run a solid race but there is a slight worry about this step up in trip.

Stratum won over hurdles last month and has to enter calculations for Willie Mullins, while it will be interesting to see which way Dubawi Fifty goes in the betting on his return from a lay-off.

He was well-fancied for this last year before meeting with a setback.

Proschema and Speedo Boy could also go well, but they were no match for RED GALILEO at Newmarket three weeks ago.

Saeed bin Suroor's charge doesn't win very often, but the penny dropped at HQ and he won with something to spare.

Tom Greatrex's claim negates his 5lb penalty and he should get a lovely tow into the race with several front-runners in attendance.

VERDICT: Win - Red Galileo Each-way - Dubawi Fifty

Race 5 - 4.10 Betfair Handicap

HOULTON is better than he showed at Doncaster last time out.

He will relish this return to an artificial surface and the booking of Andrea Atzeni catches the eye.

Archive was foiled in his bid to land the four-timer last month but he could bounce back, while Rashdan is interesting for each-way purposes.

VERDICT: Win - Houlton Each-way - Rashdan

Race 6 - 4.45 Betfair Casino EBF Novice Stakes

The market will likely prove informative here, but HURCLE could be worth chancing.

His trainer has had a sensational year with his two-year-olds and this one is bred to be pretty sharp.

Aysar is another debutant to note, while Teenar took a step in the right direction last time and has place claims.

VERDICT: Win - Hurcle Each-way - Teenar

Race 7 - 5.15 Betfair Casino Handicap

WOVEN ran better than the bare result when fourth on his return to action at Chelmsford.

The drop back to 7f from a mile is a plus and he gets weight all round here.

Mubhij is feared most for the in-form Roger Varian team, though Vale Of Kent is eased in grade after a good run at Ascot and also enters calculations.

VERDICT: Win - Woven Each-way - Vale Of Kent

Race 8 - 5.50 Betfair Casino Always A Chance Handicap

The lightly-raced HOWMAN makes his seasonal reappearance off a tempting mark.

The well-bred four-year-old should have more to offer this campaign and this 1m2f trip could prove his optimum.

Silvestre De Sousa is a good booking for Anything Today who must go close, while Rose Tinted Spirit will have improved for his recent outing and can show up well today.

VERDICT: Win - Howman Each-way - Rose Tinted Spirit
