Publish date: 2024-10-02

Deontay Wilder

Deontay Wilder is an American boxer who fights at heavyweight. He fought Tyson Fury twice in 2018 – which was a draw – and 2019 – which was won by Fury. Wilder is set to face Joseph Parker on December 23, 2023 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.
