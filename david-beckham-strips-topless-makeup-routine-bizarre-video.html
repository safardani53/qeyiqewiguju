David Beckham strips topless and shows off his makeup routine in bizarre video

Publish date: 2024-09-07

DAVID Beckham has stripped topless and shown off his makeup routine in a new video.

The dad-of-four amused fans on TikTok as he took over his wife Victoria’s social media channel and sat applying his glam.

Jokingly, David explained: “So when I use a little bit of this, my wife tells me this is the best lighting to do content,” as he wiped a makeup brush across his face.

Wearing nothing but a towel with his hair wet, the 48-year-old continued: “I tend to agree but I don’t really know what I’m talking about.

“I like to moisturise my skin and look after my skin and apparently this is the best way to do it according to Victoria Beckham beauty.”

As he finished off his humorous routine, the footballer sat back to admire himself in the clip.

Fans were amused, as one wrote in the comments: “Blend it like Beckham.”

A second added: “The tutorial we didn’t know we needed,” and a third shared: “There’s not even product on that makeup brush,” followed by a string of laughing emojis.

David’s funny clip came shortly after his 49-year-old fashion designer wife showed off how she highlights her face and applies eye shadow in a video for her fans.

David is no stranger to mocking his wife – just last month Victoria ditched her very strict diet for her birthday, and David poked fun at her.
