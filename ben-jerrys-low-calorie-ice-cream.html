Ben &amp; Jerrys has launched three new LOW CALORIE ice-cream flavours

Publish date: 2024-09-04

ICE CREAM brand Ben & Jerry's are famous for their deliciously indulgent ice-cream packed full of goodies like cookie dough and chocolate brownie.

But, as delicious as it is, they are not know for keeping things low calorie.

Well that's all about to change as the popular ice cream is getting a bit more health conscious.

It's now launched a low-calorie line, Moo-Phoria, with three flavours - Chocolate Milk & Cookies, Caramel Cookie Fix and P.B. Dough.

Each of the tubs have fewer calories, less sugar and fat and are made with organic dairy.

Each serving will have around 150 calories and 15g of sugar with the fat content reduced by 60-70 per cent.

They hit the store on February 7, so get moo-ving if you want to give them a try.
