Cory Gunz Biography

Publish date: 2024-05-14

It seems like Cory is more focused on his career rather than finding love of his life. He is living a single life. He was once in love with some of his girlfriend and from that love life, he became father of a twin daughters Madison and Mckenzie.

As his father Peter is also a well known rapper, Cory was influenced by his father to rap. When he was just 14, he signed Casablanca Records. Later he joined The Island Def Jam Music Group. In 2005, he appeared in the Rihanna's song If It's Lovin' that You Want. He has also been recording mix tapes since that time on. His first mix tape, The Apprentice Mixtape Vol. 1 was released at the end of 2005. His net worth is said to be around $125 thousand.
