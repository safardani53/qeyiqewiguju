A Rundown of His Ex-Girlfriends

Publish date: 2024-06-29

Travis Kelce was previously linked to Kayla Nicole, but he has recently been spotted with Taylor Swift. Who has Travis dated, and is he with Taylor?

The Gist:

  • Travis Kelce starred on the E! reality dating show called Chasing Kelce in 2016.
  • His was previously linked to Kayla Nicole.
  • Travis and Taylor Swift were spotted leaving a September 2023 football game together, leading many to assume they are dating.

Kansas City Chiefs tight end Travis Kelce has made his fair share of headlines lately, partly because of his spectacular performance on the field. The other reason? Travis is now being linked to American pop and country music singer Taylor Swift.

Although Taylor and Matty Healy reportedly dated sometime around May 2023, it appears the "I Can See You" singer has moved on.

While plenty of Travis's teammates and friends have settled down, Travis hasn’t taken that trip down the aisle yet. Here’s what we know about the NFL player’s romantic past and present — and maybe even his future.

Article continues below advertisement

Travis Kelce’s dating history includes breaking up and making up with Kayla Nicole.

Travis doesn’t seem to share too much about his personal life on his Instagram. However, according to Page Six, the pro athlete was previously linked to Kayla Nicole (they go as far back as 2017), although the pair is no longer together.

The sports star spoke about Kayla in December 2020, telling TMZ, "She's the best. She's the absolute best." So who is the lucky lady who snagged Travis’s heart over the course of five years? According to Us Weekly, she’s a graduate of Pepperdine University who now works as an interviewer of professional athletes.

The couple met on social media, with Kayla, who has also worked as a model, revealing, "He had been following me and insta-flirting for a few months.” They frequently hit red carpets together, and she has even developed a close friendship with Brittany, with the two WAGS previously working out together as recently as January of 2023. More on why that's somewhat scandalous in a moment. First, Travis has a very interesting past when it comes to finding women.

Before pairing off with Kayla, Travis was on a reality dating show.

Back in 2016, Travis starred on an E! reality show called Chasing Kelce. The NFL star found love on the competition series, choosing Kentucky native Maya Benberry in the end zone. Although he told his soon-to-be girlfriend she seemed somewhat arrogant and said during the finale, “I have been attracted to women like that my entire life, and those relationships didn't work because of that. I don't want that anymore.” Travis ultimately walked away with Maya, per E! News.

But after eight months, the reality romance had fizzled, and Travis quickly moved on with Kayla. Now, fans suspect he is moving on again, having reportedly called things off with Kayla in the fall of 2022.

Travis may be dating someone new.

We don’t know where the athlete stands on the issue of marriage, but per Republic World, Kayla previously hinted on social media that she is “ready to be a wife.”

But going to the chapel doesn't seem to be in the cards for the couple, as rumors began to spread that the Chiefs player had called it quits with Kayla at the end of 2022 — although she seemed to still be attending games in October 2022, per her Instagram — and started dating Access Hollywood’s Zuri Hall, per Barstool Sports.

Although Travis nor Zuri confirmed or denied reports that they dated, it seems Travis has his eye on someone new.

After rumors began stirring that he and Taylor were dating, the two were spotted leaving the Arrowhead Stadium in Kansas City after the Sept. 24, 2023 game. Oh, and prior to departing the stadium side-by-side, Taylor spent the evening in Travis's suite, enjoying the game beside his mom.

From the looks of it, it seems the singer and athlete could soon update their dating status from single to taken!
