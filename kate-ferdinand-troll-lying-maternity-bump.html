Pregnant Kate Ferdinand hits back at troll accusing her of lying about bump being too big for matern

Publish date: 2024-07-24

PREGNANT Kate Ferdinand has hit back at a troll who accused her of lying about her bump being too big for maternity pyjamas.

The former Towie star took to her Instagram Story earlier today to ask her followers for any recommendations on where she could get her hands on some comfy PJs because none of hers fit nicely anymore.

She asked her 1.3m followers: "I just need help with pyjamas you know when you get home and you want to put on some comfortable ones on.

"No, nothing's comfortable because my belly is too big. Can anyone help me?"

And although the mum-to-be shared some bits that were recommended, she also shared one particular response from a user that claimed Kate was lying about the fact her belly is now too big to fit in anything.

They wrote: "Surely you can get maternity pyjamas? Seems kind of fake, you must have loads of maternity stuff?"

Kate clapped back at the troll and said: "I don't normally respond to these people but this really made me laugh.

"I have better things to be doing with my time than faking that I need PJs. There are so many negative people out there - bizarre!

"Thanks for all of the lovely women that helped me..."

"No, nothing is comfortable because my belly is too big. Can anyone help me?"

The reality star and her husband Rio revealed that they're expecting their first child together on June 18, 2020.

Kate is already step-mum to Rio's children that he shared with his late wife Rebecca.

She secretly filmed the moment they told Lorenz, 13, Tate, 11, and nine-year-old Tia the happy news.

The trio jumped up and down with excitement as they were told they're getting a new brother or sister.

The married couple - who tied the knot last year - have kept the due date to themselves but it looks like it could be either autumn or winter 2020 because of her prominent bump.
