Kaia Gerber dating history: Who has the model dated?

Publish date: 2024-08-19

CINDY Crawford's daughter Kaia Gerber confirmed her relationship with Austin Butler after they made their red carpet debut.

Previously, the American Horror Story star was romantically linked to Pete Davidson and Jacob Elordi.

Who has Kaia Gerber dated?

In February 2019, Kaia Gerber and fellow model Wellington Grant sparked dating rumors when they were spotted holding hands in New York City.

By October 2019, the romance appeared to fizzle out as Kaia moved on with Pete Davidson.

At the time, a source revealed to Us Weekly: "Pete and Kaia are dating and they’ve been trying to keep their relationship somewhat low profile."

However, the model's relationship with the Saturday Night Live alum only lasted three months.

By January 2020, Kaia and Pete went their separate ways.

"We were dating for a few months. She's very young, and I'm f**king going through a lot, and it was before I went to rehab," Pete said of his split from Kaia.

The Bodies Bodies Bodies actor confessed to Charlamagne Tha God: "It's just like, she should be having fun. She shouldn't have to worry about some dude that just has issues and s**t.

"She should be enjoying her work. It just wasn't the right place or the right time at all."

He also credited Kaia's parents Cindy Crawford and Rande Gerber with assisting him through the breakup and rehab stint.

"Her parents were really helpful and stuff, so they're cool," he mentioned.

In September 2020, Kaia started dating Jacob Elordi.

In an interview with Vogue, she alluded to her relationship with the Euphoria star and said: "Being able to be with someone I trust, where we don't want anything from each other, having a safe, steady relationship like that, has really opened my eyes to the possibilities of love and what it feels like to love without conditions."

Over a year later in November 2021, Kaia and Jacob hit splitsville.

When did Kaia start dating Austin Butler?

In December 2021, Kaia appeared to move on from the breakup when she was seen with Austin Butler.

She was spotted with the Elvis actor after they attended a yoga class in Los Angeles.

An insider told PEOPLE: "She seems really happy. All of her friends think he's really cute."

On March 24, 2022, the reports were confirmed as the couple attended that year's W Magazine Best Performances party.
