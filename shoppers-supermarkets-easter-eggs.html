Publish date: 2024-09-06

WE'VE barely had a second to get over Christmas, but it looks like supermarkets are already gearing up for the next big occasion.

While Easter is still three months away, super organised stores are already flogging Easter eggs - and at extortionate prices, too.

One shocked customer named Rhiannon visited her local Co-op and was stunned to find rows of shelves already jam--packed with various chocolatey treats.

Amongst the Easter eggs on offer include everything from Creme egg to orange Twirl, Wispa and Mini Eggs.

But it wasn't just the fact that the store was selling the eggs so early that was a major talking point…it was the hefty price tag that came with them, too.

"What in the cost-of-living crisis is this?" Rhiannon asked, in a video posted to TikTok (@rhiannons_way).
