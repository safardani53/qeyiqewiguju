Who won at Daytona 2021 in Cheer event?

Publish date: 2024-09-29

NETFLIX'S documentary-style show Cheer is back on our screens for the second season.

But after months of training was the squad able to win at the Daytona 2021 event? Here's what you need to know.

Who won at Daytona 2021?

The show follows the Navarro College Cheer squad and their rival team, Trinity Valley Community College.

They both competed at the 2021 NCA Collegiate Cheer and Dance Championships in Daytona Beach that took place in April 2021.

Navarro was hoping to get their 15th title in 2021 as well as their third win since 2018 but in the end, Trinity Valley's cheer team was the big winner.

Where did Cheer's Navarro come at Daytona 2021?

The award-winning cheerleading team from Corsicana, Texas, led by coach Monica Aldama, came second in the competition with a score of 98.0708, according to TMZ.

The winner Trinity Valley finished with a score of 98.2292- a 0.1584 difference.

During the Cheer season two finale, Navarro had a perfect score during the prelims, while Trinity Valley had two mistakes.

At the finals, Trinity Valley had a perfect score, with Navarro seeing reductions for one mistake.

At the end the Navarro squad can be seen bursting into tears as their coach is trying to encourage them.

She said: “Y’all put your chins up. Y’all had a great performance two days in a world. ]

"Y’all worked so hard. You can’t regret anything. You put the work in.

“Y’all when you walk out of here, you can’t be disappointed in the work you put in.

"I’m telling you, we worked our tails off. We did everything we could.

"But we all know it’s cheerleading, so anything can happen.

"Be proud of the work we put in and the routine we did and hold your heads high. That’s how I expect you to act.” 

What cheer events has Navarro won?

The Navarro College Cheer Team, has won 14 National Championships since 2000.

The team won its fifth NCA Grand National Championship since 2012.

The next NCA & NDA College Nationals are scheduled for April 6, 2022.
