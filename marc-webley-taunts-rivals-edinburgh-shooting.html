Slain gunman Marc Webley taunts gangland rivals before being shot dead weeks after Jane Park split

Publish date: 2024-08-06

SLAIN hood Marc Webley taunted gangland rivals to "come and get it" before he was shot dead.

Webley posted a series of videos gauding his enemies weeks before he was gunned down on Hogmanay.

The first shows him walking on the street in Edinburgh wearing a Christmas t-shirt which reads “Ho-Ho no”.

He is also carrying a “man bag” which he hints has a weapon inside.

Webley brazenly says: “What’s in the man bag, come and find out. F*****g balloons. Come and get it, tick tick f***** boom, haha.

“Stop shooting windows ya fat p**f.”

He recorded another outside a boozer where he taunts his rivals to come and get him.

He says: “You could’ve come to the pub. I’ll only be here a couple of hours.

“Surely gives you enough time, you’ve had all day to make an effort.

“You talk a good fight, so when you are lying in bed or talking s**** or whatever you are doing, saying ‘we’re going to get him, we’re going to get him’, well here I fam. Stone cold sober.

“Come and get it. I don’t think you’ll come, you’ve not go the f*****g balls. Fire guns? Aye at windows, I’ve never missed.

“Enjoy your night guys, I will. Come and get it. We put the city on the map, you deal with the cops.”

Another video continues to tease his enemies.

He says: “Still at the pub, wee bit drunk so it would be an advantage. A good advantage.

“I don’t see it though. No honestly I don’t. Probably get me a wee tuppence. And you get a strike, that ends the show.

“We were brought up with stab wounds. You maim people, don’t want to kill anyone. I don’t blame you for that.

“You’re just no like us Roysters. We were taught from wild, wild men, one shot can end it all.

“Bit cringe, I hate doing social media but I just like to call you c***s out coz you’re f*****g chickens.

“Here myself too, you will have five bodies in a car. F*****g come, you will get me.

“I’ll get one of you though, one of you will come to the grave with me, believe it. Neck shots all round.

"Ask your boss if you can come, stay off the crack guys.

We told how Webley was shot dead just minutes before the bells on Hogmanay.

The hood is understood to have been in a feud with a well-known figure in the south of Edinburgh.

A source said: “It had been bubbling away for a while with threats from both sides. Thing is the other guy was in Spain when this happened.

“Webley left the Anchor Inn just before the bells and was shot outside. It seems like it was by someone waiting who knew he’d be there.

"Webley is a regular in there so it was fair to assume he’d be there on Hogmanay."

His Lotto winning ex Jane Park posted a heartfelt message stating she was absolutely devastated.

She posted a picture of her with Webley to her Instagram account, saying:  "I can't believe I'm writing this. We spoke yesterday.

"If I could cuddle you again and tell you everything is going to be ok, I would give anything to do it Marc.

"The months we spent together you taught me some valuable things in life and I'll never ever forget you. I am absolutely devastated."

Murder cops launched a probe into the killing yesterday.

Detective Superintendent Graham Grant said: “This is believed to have been a targeted incident and our thoughts and condolences are with the family and friends of both men involved.

“Enquiries are at a very early stage to establish the full circumstances surrounding what happened, and while officers have already gathered CCTV and spoken to various people who were in the area at the time, I’d urge anyone with information who hasn’t yet spoken to us to come forward.

“In particular if anyone has private CCTV, doorbell footage or dashcam of the Granton Crescent/Road area around the time of the incident, please bring it to our attention.
