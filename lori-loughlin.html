Lori Loughlin Biography

Publish date: 2024-08-09

In 1989, Lori got married to Michael Burns. Michael Burns is an investment banker. He is also famous for being the entertainment executive and Vice Chairman of Lionsgate. As a married couple, they lived together for about seven years, and then she got divorced in 1996.

In 1995, Lori met Mossimo Giannulli. Mossimo Giannulli is a fashion designer. He is also well known in the fashion world as the creator of the Mossimo clothing line. They got married on the 25th of November 1997. As a married couple, they have been parents twice.

During her childhood, Lori attended Hauppauge High School. As a child, she was fascinated by acting. At the age of 11, she began her career as a model. In 1971, she also appeared in the television series, The Smith Family. She made her formal acting debut in 1979 and that was in the TV movie, Too Far to Go. Next year, she appeared in the television series, The Edge of Night, and then in 1983, she made her feature film debut starring the role of Susan Baxter in the movie, Amityville 3-D. Her net worth is estimated to be around $6 million.
