Nicky Whelan Biography

Publish date: 0001-01-01

In 2014 when Nicky was starring in the 2014 movie, Left Behind she fell in love with her fellow actor, Chad Michael Murray. Chad Michael Murray is also a retired model, spokesperson, and writer. They dated each other for about half a year.

Nicky has been active in acting since 2003. That year she appeared in the television series, Pizza where she played the role of Cheerleader. The following year, she appeared in the TV movie, Russell Coight's Celebrity Challenge. In 2006, she portrayed the role of Heidi "Pepper" Steiger in the TV series, Neighbours. She is also popular for her role in the movies like Halloween II, Hollywood, Wine, Left Behind, and so on. Her net worth is estimated to be around 4.3 million dollars.
