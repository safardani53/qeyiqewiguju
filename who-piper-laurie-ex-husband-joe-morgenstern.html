Who is Piper Laurie's ex-husband, Joe Morgenstern?

Publish date: 2024-05-06

ICONIC actress Piper Laurie, who made a mark in Hollywood and nabbed numerous Oscars nominations has died at the age of 91.

Her ex-husband, Joe Morgenstern, announced on Saturday, October 14, 2023, that the Carrie actress passed away.

Who was Piper Laurie?

Piper Laurie, born January 22, 1932, was an American actress best known for her roles in Carrie, The Hustler and Twin Peaks.

The Oscar-nominated actress, born Rosetta Jacobs, broke out onto the film scene when she starred in the 1950 American comedy Louisa, alongside Ronald Reagan.

She made a splash in Hollywood when she starred as Margaret in Brian De Palma's horror classic, Carrie. The 1976 film was based off Stephen King's book. Piper played Carrie's religious mother. Her performance nabbed her an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress.

She was also nominated for the Best Actress Oscar for her role as Sarah Packard in the 1961 film, The Hustler.

In 1986, she was nominated for another Best Supporting Actress Oscar for her role in Children of a Lesser God.

Her final film performance was in 2018 in White Boy Rick.

Besides films, she also starred in a number of television films and series including The Thorn Birds, St. Elsewhere and Skag.

She played a big role in the 1990 series, Twin Peaks. She played Catherine Martell. She won a Golden Globe for her performance in the sci-fi series.

Who is Piper Laurie's ex-husband, Joe Morgenstern?

Piper married entertainment critic Joe Morgenstern in 1962. The two adopted a daughter, Anne Grace.

She left the film industry completely after her marriage to raise her family in New York. However, she was ready to act in the 1970's and starred in a Broadway version of the Tennessee Williams play, The Glass Menagerie.

Joe and Piper divorced in 1981. He confirmed her death.

At the time of this writing, it's not immediately clear what Piper's cause of death was. She was 91 at the time of her death.

What awards has Piper Laurie won?

Piper was nominated for three Oscars but never won.

She was nominated for a number of Primetime Emmy Awards. She won for playing Annie Gilbert in Promise.

She won a Golden Globe for her role on Twin Peaks.

She was nominated for a BAFTA Award in 1962.
