I did a full face of Poundland make-up to see what the fuss was about, here are all the high-end dup

Publish date: 2024-07-16

A MAKEUP fan has shared a full face tutorial of Poundland makeup and even she was surprised by some of the high-end dupes she was able to find.

In a video online Alishba was excited to try out her finds.

She said: “Full face of Primark makeup let's hope I don’t break out and let's see if any of these products are dupes for high end brands. I'm excited for that.”

First for the base foundation. 

Alishba said: “We're starting off with the Illuminating Foundation. So this kind of gives me Charlotte Tilbury vibes. I got the shade Olive. This is actually blending in quite nicely. It’s giving a glow to the skin."

Alishba was convinced the Poundland Illuminating Foundation was a dupe for the Charlotte Tilbury Light Wonder Foundation which cost £39.

She said: “It looks like the Charlotte Tilbury Wonder skin, the light foundation, and I'm telling you, it's like a dupe on my skin right now. I'm not being dramatic, I used to work for Charlotte Tilbury and I'm getting the same finish and look at that glow.”

Alishba went in next with concealer. 

She said: "I don't know if this is gonna be the right shade for me. Oh, this looks a bit too light."

Even though the concealer was too light, Alishba gave her verdict.
