Kimberly Guilfoyle Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-09-23

Bio:
Kimberly Guilfoyle (born Kimberly Ann Guilfoyle in San Francisco on March 9, 1969) is a TV news personality and was one of the co-hosts of the Fox News shows The Five and Outnumbered before leaving her positions in July 2018. She has also contributed to the programs The O’Reilly Factor, Hannity and Red Eye w/ Greg Gutfeld. Before these gigs, she was an anchor on Court TV and a legal analyst/commentator for Fox News and ABC.

Best Known For:
Kimberly Guilfoyle is best known for being the co-host of Fox News Channel’s The Five and Outnumbered. She made headlines in October 2014 for asserting on The Five that young women shouldn’t be allowed to vote or serve on juries.

Personal Life:
Kimberly Guilfoyle’s ex-husband is current Lieutenant Governor and former mayor of San Francisco Gavin Newsom. She was also married to furniture heir Eric Villency, with whom she has one young child, Ronan Anthony. She is now dating Donald Trump Jr.
