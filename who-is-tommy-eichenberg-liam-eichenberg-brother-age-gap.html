Who Is Tommy Eichenberg, Liam Eichenberg Brother? Age Gap

Publish date: 2024-03-29

Liam Eichenberg has a younger brother named Tommy Eichenberg, and they used to play high school football together.

The last time they played football for the same team was when Liam was a senior at St. Ignatius High School, and Tommy was a freshman.

The two brothers share a close bond and are very supportive of one another.

Liam was a role model to his younger brother and was the reason why Tommy played football.

Liam Eichenberg, born on January 19, 1998, is an American football center for the Miami Dolphins of the NFL.

The Cleveland, Ohio native attended Saint Ignatius High School and played in the 2016 Under Armour All-American Game.

After an impressive high school career, he committed to play for the Notre Dame Fighting Irish football team in 2015.

Despite a shaky beginning, he chose to redshirt his first year and served as a backup, playing in only five games during his second year.

However, he eventually gained his footing and secured the position of starting left tackle in 2018.

Furthermore, he started his professional NFL career as the second-round pick of the 2021 NFL draft by the Dolphins.

Liam Eichenberg Brother: Tommy Eichenberg Is A Collegiate Football Player

Liam has a brother named Tommy Eichenberg, born on January 11, 2001, who is three years younger than him.

He attended the same high school as his elder brother and played football with him.

Following his high school career, he got offers from several colleges, including Ohio State, Boston College, and Iowa.

Initially, the linebacker committed to play college football at Boston College but reopened his recruitment during his senior year of high school.

Tommy ultimately signed to play at Ohio State as a four-star recruit. As of this writing, he is a fifth-year senior who graduated with a real estate degree in May 2023.

Moreover, with his impressive performance, Eichenberg was named the defensive MVP of the 2022 Rose Bowl.
