Torrei Hart Pics Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Torrei Hart is seen attendng boxing legend Floyd Mayweather’s 43rd Birthday Celebration. The exclusive bash was held at the Sunset Eden in Los Angeles on Feb. 21, 2020. Torrei rocked a stunning floral print Ulla Johnson mini dress for the event, along with drop earrings and a high ponytail.

Torrei Hart was born on February 28th, 1978, in Philadelphia, and was later raised in New Jersey. As a young student, she took up acting lessons and quickly became a high school star. She later met comedian Kevin Hart while studying at Community College of Philadelphia. As a couple, the pair moved to Hollywood to pursue their dreams, where the later married. Kevin and Torrei are parents to two children, Heaven and Hendrix, however, the pair divorced by 2010.

Professionally, Torrei has appeared in a number of films and television projects, including ‘Soul Plane’ — which also starred Kevin — Nickelodeon’s ‘Parental Discretion’ and more. She also started her own successful YouTube comedy channel called PrettyFunnyFish.
