Who is Adam Gase:

Adam Gase is a famous Football Coach. He was born on March 29, 1978 and his birthplace is Ypsilanti, MI. Adam is also well known as, Offensive minded coach who got his first opportunity as a head coach with the Miami Dolphins in 2016. They relieved him of his duties in the 2018 off-season, and he quickly signed with Dolphins rival the New York Jets as their head ball coach.

Adam is originated from United States. He has worked under coaches like Mike Martz and John Fox.
