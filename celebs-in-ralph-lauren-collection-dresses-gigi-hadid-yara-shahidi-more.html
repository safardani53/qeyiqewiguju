Celebs in Ralph Lauren Collection Dresses: Gigi Hadid, Yara Shahidi, More

Publish date: 2024-03-30

When you hear the name Ralph Lauren you may think of preppy plaid polos, the brand’s famous bear emblazoned on sweaters or maybe the uniforms he designed for 2018 Winter Olympics in Pyeonchang. But the Ralph Lauren Collection, which debuted in 1971, is what Hollywood is buzzing about. The iconic American designer’s high-end pieces consists of embellished, figure-flattering gowns — and the stars can’t get enough! This season, his standout styles feature interesting fabrications, like a liquid silk (rocked by Jennifer Hudson) and a glossy mikado (worn by Nina Dobrev). It’s a whole different vibe from the original line that’s edgier, slinkier and more playful. And to say it’s been turning heads recently on the red carpet is an understatement — Hollywood officially approves!
