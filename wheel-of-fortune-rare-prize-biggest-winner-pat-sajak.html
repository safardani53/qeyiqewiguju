Wheel of Fortune player Preston holds back tears after he wins giant prize as fans go wild at rare f

Publish date: 2024-07-27

WHEEL of Fortune contestant Preston Leslie nearly spun into tears after winning the biggest money pile this season.

Longtime host, Pat Sajak, 76, revealed that Preston chose the $100,000 prize card as a blast of confetti and fans on Twitter erupted.

The gymnastics coach from Seattle, Washington lunged to the Wheel of Fortune bonus round with the highest score.

He had earned $26,000 and a trip to Costa Rica.

Preston chose "Phrase," and with some helpful additional letters, the three-word puzzle only had three letters left.

"Just say it," host Pat relented as it looked like Preston would stick the landing.

When the 10-second timer began, Preston immediately guessed: “Bust a move?”

Pat deemed him correct and then revealed his prize card wasn’t the expected $40,000, but the $100,000, the biggest this season.

Streamers burst onto the stage as Preston nearly burst into tears and was speechless after his $126,000 total.

'OH MY GOD!'

Preston could barely get out an "Oh my God!" as he hugged his partner of 14 years, and Vanna White, 65, who came over to the wheel to congratulate the contestant.

They all rejoiced as confetti filled the stage.

In a post-credits scene, with Preston adorned with streamers, Pat hilariously admitted he had never heard of the winning phrase.

"'Bust a move,' what does that mean?" Pat asked.

'100K WINNER!'

Fans went into a frenzy on Twitter where one rejoiced: "A $100,000 win finally!"

"A guy just won $100,000 on Wheel of Fortune. I got chills!" wrote another.

"My old tumbling coach winning Wheel of Fortune," rejoiced a third who seemed to know Preston from back home.

"$100,000 winner on Wheel of Fortune!!! Wooooooo!" wrote a fourth.

One more claimed: "It was nearly a year ago when Ilene won the big one on January 25th, 2022.

We knew tonight was the night that we got the first $100,000 winner in Season 40! Congratulations, Preston!"

'LEAVE A CARD'

Pat has made it no secret that he and co-host, Vanna White, are nearing the end.

Their contracts are up next season, and on Monday's episode, a contestant named Eric tried to help his decision about a potential successor.

"Pat, listen, you’ve been doing this for 40 years, and I would love to have your job," the enthusiastic contestant exclaimed.

"As we say in show business, the show must go on. And I want the show to go on with me."

As the audience gasped at the notion, Pat completely froze in place, looking as though his soul left his body.

The TV star finally asked: "Do you know something I don't?" before he added, "No, it's good to have ambitions."

Pat then told the contestant to “just leave a card,” with the official Wheel of Fortune Instagram noting that Eric did, indeed, leave his card.

Fans are now wondering if they'll renew their contracts or if a certain someone will replace Pat.
