Publish date: 2024-09-25

WITH the exception of lockdown, we've been going to Nando's at least once a month for as long as we can remember - so let's just say, our go-to meal is something we've spent years perfecting.

But when we're ordering our garlic peri-peri chicken butterfly burger with a side of chips, we've never once thought to question forking out an extra 65p for the mouth-watering "Perinaise".

He then recommended mixing the condiment with one of the free sauces the restaurant has on offer.
