Who was Craig Sjodin? | The US Sun

Publish date: 2024-05-22

CRAIG Sjodin had a successful career working behind the scenes in the entertainment industry.

The photographer has tragically passed away at the age of 67.

Who was Craig Sjodin?

Craig Oliver Sjodin was born in Evanston, Illinois, on October 26, 1955, and died on September 15, 2023 in California, per his obituary.

He worked as a still photographer for ABC TV and its acquirer, Disney, for nearly four decades.

During that time, he worked for hit reality shows such as The Bachelor and its spinoffs, as well as the popular soap opera, General Hospital.

Craig was also a "seasoned bicyclist" who frequently logged "hundreds of miles a week."

He cycled in places such as San Diego and Westlake Village, California.

Craig retired from his job in August 2023 and was planning on traveling to Australia and "other sites he visited over the course of his career" with his family.

What was his cause of death?

According to his obituary, Craig died from injuries he sustained in a bicycle accident.

Following the accident, he was taken to Los Robles Hospital and Medical Center before tragically passing eight days later.

His obituary thanks "the family and friends who rushed to his side" at the hospital and "who sat with him through those agonizing eight days before his passing."

It also thanks those friends and family members for "continuing to comfort his wife, son, sisters and relatives through this tragic ordeal."

Craig is survived by "his loving wife" of 30 years, Esther Bekhore Sjodin, as well as his son, Ryan.

He is also survived by his sisters, including Brenda (Dave) Rickey, Nancy Nickoloff, and Linda Hughes, as well as his niece Kerri (Jeff) Lane and grand-nieces Avery and Grace Lane, "among other relatives."

His obituary continues: "The family will miss Craig's companionship on our long bike rides, our marathon pickleball sessions, his unquenchable thirst for Arnold Palmer drinks, his sense of humor and infectious laughter.

"He was the youngest 67-year-old we have ever known."

How long did Craig Sjodin work for ABC?

Craig worked for ABC for 39 years, per his obituary.

According to Soaps.com, throughout his photography work for General Hospital, he "shot everyone who was anyone in Port Charles — and on countless primetime series as well."

The September 27, 2023 episode of the hit soap ended with an "In Loving Memory" card for Craig.

Referring to his work on The Bachelor and its spin-offs, his obituary states: "If you have seen the images of contestants holding their roses at the beginning of each season, you probably have seen Craig's work over the years."
