'EPIC NIGHT!'

Before showing off her figure on live TV, Hoda shared that she had attended Taylor Swift's concert in New Jersey over the weekend.

"@njtransit was the only way to get to @taylorswift Epic concert friday night!! Xo @jennifermillerjewelry @jolamathisen #shari," she captioned her post.

Hoda shared some pictures in the post of her getting there and then enjoying the three-and-a-half hour concert with friends.

In the first photo, the host took a selfie with all the so-called Swifties she met in the train station.

Then, it was everyone getting onto the train, and another selfie with fans.

She and three other friends finally found their seats and smiled in more shots.

Hoda snapped a photo of them together on the floor during Taylor's Lover set.

The 58-year-old then ended her post with a video where she panned across the whole stadium.

Taylor was performing a song from her album, evermore, and the fans lit up their phones' flashlights.

LEATHER LADY

Although Hoda kept her look casual at the Taylor Swift concert, with just a denim jacket, T-shirt, and pants, she shocked fan by wearing a sexy look in May.

At the 2023 Webby Awards, she wore a tight, leather dress.

The Today host was nominated for a Webby Award for her podcast Making Space with Hoda Kotb.

The 27th annual ceremony took place in New York City.

Hoda ended up winning the award while at the event.

Winners are asked to give five-word acceptance speeches when they receive an award.

"OK, here are my five," Hoda said when she came on stage.

She exclaimed her five words: "Listen. Love, love, love. Bye!"

Hoda was clad in a tight black leather dress that accentuated her curves.

The lengthy frock hugged her frame and made her the center of attention.

She paired the dress with minimalistic jewelry and toned-down makeup.

The leather look did all the talking for Hoda, so additional accessories weren't a must.
