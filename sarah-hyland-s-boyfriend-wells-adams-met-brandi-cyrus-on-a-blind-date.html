Sarah Hyland's Boyfriend Wells Adams Met Brandi Cyrus on a Blind Date

Publish date: 2024-04-08

Secret boxes are opening! Wells Adams, who is currently in a relationship with Sarah Hyland, revealed on Friday, January 18, that he met Brandi Cyrus on a blind date but it didn't end in romance.

The  alum, 34, and Miley Cyrus' sister, 31, have been co-hosting podcast since December 2018 but the podcast is not the place where their friendship began.

Adams told media at the first annual live iHeartRadio Podcast Awards Presented by Capital One, "We got set up on a blind date. It did not work.

Brandi added, "Just friend vibes, man".

When Adams said Brandi "doesn’t like funny guys", Brandi responded, "I like to be friends with funny guys, but I don’t want to date a funny guy. I like quiet guys."

The actor then added, "The date went well enough where we’re like, ‘Oh well, this is cool.’ … So it wasn’t terrible".

The pop star continued,

Long story short, we were set up on a date, hung out a couple times. Wells has a couple radio shows in Nashville, so I started coming in to guest on his morning show and then we were like, ‘Let’s start a podcast! This is fun.’ And here we are, two years later.

The ABC personality and Modern Family actress Sarah Hylan, 28, first sparked dating rumors after they attended Brandi's Halloween party together in 2017. The duo was dressed as ‘ Eleven and Dustin at the time.

Earlier this year, Adams revealed that he and his girlfriend are considering marriage, While promoting his Pratt Cast podcast with Stephanie Pratt, he said at the time, "We are not engaged. But … it’s definitely going to happen."
