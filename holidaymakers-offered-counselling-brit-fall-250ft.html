Holidaymakers who saw Brit fall 250ft to his death at Benidorm hotel offered counselling

Publish date: 2024-04-26

HOLIDAYMAKERS who saw a Brit fall 250ft to his death at a hotel have been offered counselling.

Witnesses claim there was a row involving the man and his wife before the tragedy at Benidorm’s Flamingo Oasis.

Rachael Gamsby, 30, was in her first-floor room with mum Mary, 56, when they heard a bang and then saw the body.

Security guard Rachael, of Birmingham, said: “We’re still traumatised and will never forget it.”

Tui has offered support after guests asked for refunds or to switch hotel.

Spanish authorities have yet to release details of the dead man.

But sources claimed he was a 45-year-old from Newport, Gwent.

He had been drinking on his flight from Cardiff and in the hotel bar before going to his 24th-floor room last Tuesday.
