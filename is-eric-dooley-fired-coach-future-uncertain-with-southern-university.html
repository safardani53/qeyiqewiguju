Is Eric Dooley Fired? Coach Future Uncertain With Southern University

Publish date: 2024-06-12

There has been speculation among fans about the future of Eric Dooley, the head coach of Southern University’s football team. The question remains: has Eric Dooley been fired?

While some sources suggest that he might be relieved of his duties after the Bayou Classic, reports from WBRZ’s Michael Cauble indicate that Dooley has not been fired yet.

He has confirmed that he hasn’t had any discussions with administrators regarding his employment status.

Despite this, the university has not officially addressed the situation, leaving fans and supporters of the team wondering about the fate of their coach.

Fans and supporters of the team are eager to know if the coach will continue to lead the team or if there will be a new face on the sidelines for Southern University’s football program.

Is Eric Dooley Fired?

Eric Dooley, the head coach at Southern University, hasn’t been confirmed as fired yet.

The situation remains uncertain as neither the university nor Eric has addressed the issue officially.

Dooley’s coaching journey at Southern University has seen ups and downs. Currently, the team stands at 5-5, no longer in the running for the SWAC West title.

However, in his first year as head coach, the team went 7-5, securing the SWAC West title and making it to the SWAC championship game against Jackson State.

While there’s been some promise under Dooley’s leadership, inconsistency and a lack of progress have put his job at risk.

He arrived at Southern University in 2021, which was anticipated as a return to his coaching roots, having previously worked under Pete Richardson.

His contract with Southern University is a four-year deal worth $310,000 annually, with a possible fifth year.

However, the situation remains unclear, with no official statement from the university regarding Dooley’s future.

WBRZ’s Michael Cauble stated that Dooley confirmed he hasn’t discussed his employment status with administrators.

There’s speculation that changes might occur soon, although school officials have provided no official updates.

Eric Dooley Career And Networth

Eric Dooley was born on July 14, 1965. He is a former football player and a renowned coach.

Currently, he serves as the head coach for the Southern Jaguars Football program, located in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.

Throughout his journey in the coaching world, Dooley has achieved significant milestones.

Prior to his appointment at Southern University, he held positions at Prairie View A&M University, where his contributions were remarkable. 

Before Prairie View A&M, Dooley made a name for himself at Grambling State University, where he served as the offensive coordinator. 

Apart from his coaching achievements, Dooley also has an impressive background as a player.

He played wide receiver at Grambling State, winning two SWAC Championships. He holds a bachelor’s degree from Southern University New Orleans and a master’s degree from Southern University Baton Rouge.

Dooley is also actively involved in off-field activities, including his membership in the American Football Coaches Association and Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Incorporated.
