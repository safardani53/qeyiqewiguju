Channing Tatum's sexuality garnered a great deal of attention over the years. So, what is his sexuality? We have all the details inside.

Rumors about Channing Tatum's sexual orientation have been making the rounds for quite some time now. Although the phenomenal actor and dancer was mainly linked to women in the past, this didn't stop fans from all walks of life to spread rumors about his sexuality. Now that Channing is reportedly single, it's time to take another look at his sexual identity. So, what's there to know about his sexuality?
