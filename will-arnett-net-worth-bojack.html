Who is Will Arnett?

Will Arnett, born May 4, 1970, is a Canadian actor and comedian.

Arnett was born in Toronto, Ontario, and briefly attended Lakefield College School, but was asked to leave after a semester.

He then graduated from Leaside High School and headed to Concordia University in Canada, but ended up dropping out shortly.

Arnett later moved to New York City in 1990 to study acting at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.

Arnett is best known for his appearance in Arrested Development, which earned him a Primetime Emmy Award nomination.

Acclaimed for his great voice, Arnett exceeds in voice acting and stars in many animated films including Despicable Me, Ratatouille, and Ice Age: The Meltdown.

His legendary voice in the hit show, BoJack Horseman, won him an Annie Award at the 46th Annie Awards.

Arnett is continuing his acting journey with Netflix's new show, Murderville.

The comedy series premiered on February 3, 2022, and stars prominent guests, including Conan O'Brien, Sharon Stone, and Ken Jeong.

What is Will Arnett's net worth?

Celebrity Net Worth suggests the actor's net worth is approximately $35million.

Arnett is reported to earn $125,000 per episode and has starred in more than 50 films and television shows.

He sold his house in Beverly Hills for $8million in 2021, then purchased a new home in the same city for $16.4million.

Is Will Arnett married?

Arnett married Penelope Ann Miller, a fellow actor, in 1994, and divorced just a year later in 1995.

The actor then married Amy Poehler on August 29, 2003.

They have two sons together, but eventually separated in 2012 and divorced in July 2016.

Arnett told the Guardian in February 2022 that he once broke down in tears on his way to set, as the separation with Poehler was "just brutal, brutal, brutal."

With almost 10 years since the split, Arnett is now happily dating one of the Hottest Socialites of 2017, Alessandra Brawn, and welcomed their first child, Alexander, in May 2020.
