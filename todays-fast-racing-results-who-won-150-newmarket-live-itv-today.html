OASIS CHARM won the 1:50 Spring Lodge Stakes at Newmarket.

In a terrific battle in the final stages with Sharja Bridge, the Godolphin runner managed to pull out all the stops to hold off his persistent challenger.

Result of the 1:50 at Newmarket:

Winning trainer - Charlie Appleby
